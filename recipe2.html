Dish Name: Chicken Tikka Masala

Recipe and Ingredients

Ingredients:

  • 500g Chicken breast, cut into chunks
  • 1 large onion, finely chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tbsp ginger, grated
  • 1 tbsp garam masala
  • 1 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1 tsp cumin powder
  • 1 tsp paprika
  • 1 can (400g) diced tomatoes
  • 1/2 cup cream
  • 2 tbsp vegetable oil
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh cilantro, chopped (for garnish)

Recipe Steps:

  1. Heat vegetable oil in a large pan over medium heat.
  2. Add chopped onions and saut√© until golden brown, about 5 minutes.
  3. Stir in garlic and ginger, cooking for another 2 minutes until fragrant.
  4. Add the chicken chunks and cook for about 7 minutes, until browned.
  5. Stir in the spices: garam masala, turmeric, cumin, and paprika. Cook for another minute.
  6. Add the diced tomatoes and let the mixture simmer for 15 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  7. Pour in the cream and let it simmer for another 5-10 minutes until the sauce thickens.
  8. Season with salt to taste and garnish with fresh cilantro.
  9. Serve hot with rice or naan bread.
