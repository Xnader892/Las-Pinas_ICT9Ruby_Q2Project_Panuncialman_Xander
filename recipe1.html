Dish Name: Adobo

Recipe and Ingredients

Ingredients:

  • Chicken (or Pork) - 1 kg
  • Garlic - 5 cloves, minced
  • Soy Sauce - 1/2 cup
  • Vinegar - 1/4 cup
  • Bay Leaves - 2-3 leaves
  • Black Pepper - 1 tsp
  • Onion - 1 medium, sliced (optional)
  • Water - 1 cup
  • Oil - for frying

Recipe Steps:

  1. Heat oil in a pan and sauté garlic until golden brown.
  2. Add chicken (or pork) and sauté until lightly browned.
  3. Pour in the soy sauce and vinegar, then add bay leaves and pepper. Let it simmer for 5-10 minutes without stirring.
  4. Add water and bring to a boil, then lower the heat and let it simmer for 30-40 minutes until the meat is tender and the sauce thickens.
  5. Adjust seasoning if necessary (add salt or sugar to taste).
  6. Serve hot with steamed rice. Enjoy!
